Who we are

ATOH design is a fashion brand owned by Alliu Oluwaseun Titilayo. She Started this brand since her secondary school days and has developed the brand into what it is today. We are into different kind of fahion wears such as street wears, office wears, casual wears, beach outfits, owambe and aso ebi wears, bridal wears and many more. We are also into selling of quality materials such as; Crepe Chiffon, Ankara, Adire, Poly, Organza/Organdi, Vintage, lace. We also train people who are interested in learning fashion skill on the beginners level, intermediate level and advanced level. Some of the other service we render are pattern drafting, beading, stoning outfits, corset class and many more. To learn more about our service you can reach out to us via our contact page or book an appointment with us for consultation today.
